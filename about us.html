Trucking services refer to the transportation of goods using a truck or a fleet of trucks. These services can include a variety of different types of trucking, such as:

  • Less-than-truckload (LTL) shipping, which involves the transport of small shipments that do not require a full truck.
  • Full truckload (FTL) shipping, which involves the transport of a large shipment that fills an entire truck.
  • Flatbed trucking, which involves the transport of oversized or heavy items that cannot be transported using a standard trailer.
  • Refrigerated trucking, which involves the transport of perishable goods that require a temperature-controlled environment.
  • Hazardous materials trucking, which involves the transport of dangerous or hazardous goods that require special handling and transport regulations.
  • Express trucking, which involves the transport of time-sensitive or urgent shipments that require faster delivery.
